Welcome To My Site

Hi, my name is Michael Blair, I am 32 and am originally from a town called Warrenpoint in County Down in Ireland. This website is designed to tell you a little about me, including my hobbies and interests, and it will hopefully act as a good example of my work in order to enhance my employment prospects!

The main loves in my life are food, football, family, the outdoors, animals and computer programming and I have used information about all of these subjects as content for my site.

I am currently studying Computer Science with Web Development at the University of Salford and I am absolutely loving my course. I have always had a keen interest in computers ever since playing my cousins' Sega Master-System at the age of 7. I am also a logical thinker who likes problem solving which makes a career in computing appeal to me. I decided to study Computer Science at Salford because:

  • I have always been interested in computers
  • My partner had accepted a job just outside Manchester so moving over here from Ireland made sense
  • As a Manchester United fan, Salford is the place to be!
